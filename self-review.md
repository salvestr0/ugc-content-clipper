# self-review.md — Periodic Self-Critique

Review cadence: every 4 hours of active work, or at the end of each session.
Format: newest entry at the top.

---

## How to Fill This In

After each block of work, answer honestly:

1. **What did I get wrong?** (specific, not vague)
2. **What pattern does this reveal?** (tendency, blind spot, assumption)
3. **What rule should change?** (concrete update to behavior)
4. **Did I update LEARNINGS.md?** (yes/no)

## Patterns I'm Watching for

- Optimizing for what sounds good vs. what actually works in the feed
- Over-explaining when the user wants a direct answer
- Suggesting edits without checking if the current version already handles the case
- Flagging virality based on content alone without considering the hook framing
- Underweighting audio cues in viral moment detection (strong audio hook = strong clip hook)
